    My goals

    • Standard filters
    • List of goals
      • Displaying the goal list
      • Filtering the list

    To find goals that are available to you, apply standard filters or use additional options to configure your goal list.

    Standard filters

    1. In the left-hand panel, select Goals.

    2. To quickly find goals you're participating in, select the filter:

      • My goals for the goals where you are the author, lead, participant, or customer.

      • I'm author, I'm the lead, I'm a participant, or I'm the customer for the goals where you have the respective role.

      • Favorites for the goals that you added to Favorites.

      • Viewed recently for the goals you opened recently.

    3. If you can't see all relevant goals, expand the list:

      • To expand the list based on the set filter, click All my goals (the button name depends on the filter you selected) in the bottom left panel.

      • To open all goals available to you in Yandex Tracker, click All goals in the bottom left panel or follow this link.

    List of goals

    By default, the list shows the main goal parameters, including the name, status, progress, author, and lead.

    Goals linked to projects and portfolios are marked with the icon. Click the icon to view the list of linked projects and portfolios.

    Displaying the goal list

    To choose how goals are displayed, click one of the tabs at the top of the page:

    • Structure: Tree view that shows the links between parent and child goals (subgoals).

      Key results for goals also appear as nested items with the icon. For more information, see Adding key results.

      A goal that has subgoals but no parent is called a top-level goal. To see the structure of a specific top-level goal, add the Top-level goals filter parameter in the Structure view and specify the desired goal. For more information, see Filtering the list.

      When filters are active, their effect extends to both parent and child goals in the Structure view. For example, if a parent goal doesn't match the filters, neither it nor its child goals will appear in the list.

    • List: Flat list that doesn't show the links between goals and key results.

    Filtering the list

    To search the list for a specific goal, use the following filters:

    • To find a goal by name, type its name in the search bar.
    • To search by a specific goal parameter, click its name above the list. To add more search parameters, click Add a field for filtering and select a field.
    • To set up the goal display order, click Soring and select a parameter.

    The filter settings persist and apply after you refresh the page or open the goal list again. The filter is retained for each tab separately.

    To share your filtered goal list, copy the URL from the browser address bar.

    Click on the goal's name to open its page. You'll see goal details in the right sidebar if you've turned on the Open issues in the sidebar option in the Yandex Tracker interface settings.
